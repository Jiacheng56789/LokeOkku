Still Life Art

Description:

This is a showcase about how I done a final still life artwork with charcoal stick and charcoal
pencil but I have never used these medium to do any art before, the experience was great! I
remembered that I was not willing to use charcoal as a drawing medium, because it feels dirty
and hard to clean in my opinion. But after I touched it, it was hard to believe I already enjoy
this medium without realize by myself, the use of charcoal is good for doing still life because
of its dark spice is easy to express the shadow, and it's better than pencil! The only disadvantage
is the action CANNOT be undone because it could not erase by an erasor!
